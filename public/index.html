Aims & Mission

Pillars of Our Support

Bridging oncology medical expertise and patient welfare across West Africa.

Patient Welfare & Care

Alleviating the financial burden of treatments of patients with musculoskeletal tumours and providing social and psychological support for them and their families.

Stories of Real Impact

Sarah's Journey to Recovery

Sarah, a young mother of two, was diagnosed with osteosarcoma. Realizing that clinical expertise is only half the battle, doctors from the Lagos Musculoskeletal Oncology Network (LAMON) connected her family with MONSUF to cover the treatment expenses.

Thanks to the support of MONSUF, she underwent limb-salvage surgery and chemotherapy sessions. Today, she is cancer-free, walking, and running alongside her children.
